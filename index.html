📋 How to get your data from E*TRADE ▼ Collapse Instructions

To generate your Irish Revenue Capital Gains compliant output, the system parses the lot specifics from your historical spreadsheet ledger. Follow the steps below to export the file:

  1. Log into your E*TRADE account profile in a web browser.
  2. Click on "Stock Plan (MDB)" then navigate to My Account > Gains & Losses tab.
  3. Filter by your target Tax Year dropdown selection and hit Apply.
  4. Locate the Download link interface dropdown situated on the right side.
  5. Select Download Expanded (Crucial: do not select collapsed!).
